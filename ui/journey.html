The week, in ten beats

Portal opens

User taps the AstroPath app icon; the cosmic artwork greets them. First brand impression.

Sign in

Phone OTP is fastest in India. Email / Google / Apple / WhatsApp also available.

Onboard

Welcome → pick focus areas → choose language → enter birth details. Kundli is cast.

Dashboard

First look at their personal horoscope, energy score, moon phase, and quick actions.

Kundli

Explore the chart — houses, planets, dasha, doshas. Tap any element to learn more.

Horoscope

Switch period (day / week / month / year) and rashi. Scan love / career / finance / health.

Panchang

Check auspicious windows. Find a muhurat for marriage, business, travel, griha pravesh.

Consult

Book a real astrologer by chat, call, or video. Wallet handles billing per minute.

AI guide

Between sessions, the AI answers quick questions grounded in their own chart.

Tune settings

Switch language, manage notifications, review privacy & disclaimer. Done.
