AstroPath · Guidelines

How we read the sky — and the lines we do not cross.

AstroPath offers advisory spiritual guidance drawn from Vedic astrology. We are not a substitute for qualified medical, legal, or financial professionals. Please read these notes before you rely on any reading, consultation, or AI reply.

Terms of Use Privacy AI Disclaimer Community Consultations Refunds Wellbeing Contact

1. Terms of Use

By creating an AstroPath account you agree to use the app for personal, non-commercial guidance. You will not redistribute paid content, impersonate another user, or scrape astrologer listings. You must be 18 years or older, or have your guardian's consent, to book a paid consultation.

AstroPath content — horoscopes, kundli readings, panchang, muhurat windows, and AI replies — is provided for reflection, not as a substitute for professional judgment. Decisions you make in your life are yours; we only offer perspective.

2. Privacy

We store the minimum data needed to give you a reading: your name, birth date, birth time, birth place, language preference, and chat history with the AI assistant. Consultations with human astrologers are private between you and the astrologer; AstroPath holds a transcript only for safety review and refund resolution.

  • We never sell your birth details or chart to advertisers.
  • Your chat with the AI lives locally on your device and in short-lived server memory for conversational continuity. You can clear it from Settings or by tapping "Clear chat" in the assistant.
  • We retain kundli records so you can re-open past readings; you can delete them from Settings at any time.

3. AI Assistant Disclaimer

The AI assistant is a software layer over curated astrology content. It is not deterministic and does not forecast specific events. It will decline to give medical diagnoses, legal strategy, stock tips, or crisis counselling. If you ask about self-harm, the assistant will pause and point you to helplines.

If you need support that goes beyond spiritual guidance — a diagnosis, a contract review, a trade decision, or crisis help — please reach out to a qualified professional. The assistant will always say so itself when a topic is outside its scope.

4. Community Standards

  • Speak respectfully with astrologers. Abusive language ends the session.
  • Do not share another person's birth details without their consent.
  • Do not request readings that are coercive (to pressure a specific person into a choice).
  • Report any astrologer who promises a guaranteed outcome, a cure, or pressures you to buy gemstones off-platform.

5. Consultation Policy

Paid consultations start from your wallet balance. The per-minute rate is shown before you connect. Calls and video sessions begin a 60-second trial; if either side disconnects in that window you are not charged. Astrologers are independent practitioners; AstroPath verifies credentials but does not control their individual advice.

6. Refund Policy

Wallet top-ups are refundable within 7 days if unused. Completed consultations are non-refundable, but we will review disputes in good faith if you flag the session within 48 hours and provide a reason. Partial refunds are common for technical failures or dropped calls.

7. Wellbeing Note

Astrology can be a comforting frame, and it can also be misused to justify anxiety or inaction. If a reading leaves you feeling worse, step away. Speak to someone you trust. If you are in distress in India, iCall (9152987821) and Vandrevala Foundation (1860-2662-345) offer confidential counselling. For immediate danger, call 112.

We will never ask you to perform a remedy that harms you, costs a large sum up-front, or isolates you from family or professional support.

8. Contact & Reporting

Reach us at hello@astropath.app for general queries, or trust@astropath.app to report an astrologer, a refund issue, or a safety concern. We aim to respond within two business days.
